Delicious homemade pizza

This is a recipe for a delicious homemade kiwi pizza! This recipe has been passed down for generations by the Nguyen Dynasty! We hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Kiwi
  • Pizza Dough

Steps

  1. Lay out the pizza dough
  2. Spread pizza sauce on the dough
  3. Generously place kiwi slices on the sauce
  4. Put into the oven at 450 degrees for 20 mins
  5. Enjoy!
