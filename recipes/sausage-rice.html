Sausage and Rice

Delicious Sausage and Rice Recipe!

This is a recipe for homemade sausage and rice! This recipe has been passed down for generations by the Nguyen Dynasty! We hope you enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Rice

Steps

  1. cut sausage into bite size pieces
  2. cook sausage on a pan for 5-10 mins
  3. add sausage to premade rice
  4. Done! Enjoy!
